## Introduction

This portfolio website serves as a personal showcase of my skills, projects, and accomplishments. It was created to give potential clients or employers a better understanding of my capabilities and experience in web development and design.

You can find a selection of my best work, along with an "About Me" section, a contact form, and links to my social media profiles.

---

## Technologies Used

- **HTML5**: For structuring the content and layout of the website.
- **CSS3**: For styling the site with a responsive, clean, and modern design.
- **JavaScript**: For dynamic interactions and functionality on the website (such as form validation or animations).
- **Font Families**:
  - **Gordita**: A modern and friendly sans-serif font used for headings.
  - **Recoleta**: A stylish and elegant serif font used for body text.
- **Images**: Various high-quality images are used throughout the website to showcase projects and designs.
- **Favicon**: Custom favicon to enhance branding.

---

## Features

- **Responsive Design**: The site adapts to different screen sizes (desktops, tablets, and mobiles).
- **Interactive Elements**: Smooth scrolling, hover effects, and animations are used to engage visitors.
- **Project Gallery**: Displaying selected works and projects.
- **Contact Form**: Allows visitors to reach out for inquiries or job opportunities.
- **Social Media Links**: Quick access to my LinkedIn, GitHub, and other social media profiles.
